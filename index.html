About Me

My name is Christabel Planite. I am a wedding planning specialist located in California. I work with the knowledge, planning savvy engaged couples who want to plan independently but need a professional to tie loose ends in their final pre-wedding month and provide on-site management on their big day. As an experienced wedding planner, my promise is to plan every single detail on behalf of you at each stage and just like you would do in the desired professional way.
